Crazy FAQ's

Can a novel change my life? +
Absolutely! A great novel can open new worlds, challenge your thinking, and even inspire you to change your perspective or take action. It might even push you to start your own story!
Why don’t cats like water? +
Cats are mysterious creatures. It's likely because they prefer to stay dry and be the ultimate rulers of their domain.
Can I time travel? +
Not quite yet, but we're working on it. For now, try living in the moment and enjoying today.
Do aliens exist? +
We haven't met them yet, but the universe is vast. We wouldn't be surprised if they were out there. Just don't invite them over for dinner—yet.
Can I turn my dreams into reality… literally? +
Well, if we had the technology to do that, we’d all be living in dreamland. For now, we suggest starting with a vision board.
Do plants have their own social networks? +
Absolutely! Through their underground root systems, plants exchange nutrients and messages, possibly discussing which sunlight is better for selfies.
Can I make friends with my shadow? +
It’s always there when you need it. So yes, it’s a loyal companion! But it’s hard to get it to go on adventures.
If I sleep on a book, will I absorb its knowledge? +
Unfortunately, sleep-reading hasn’t been scientifically proven—yet. But you might have some great book-themed dreams!
If I read a book backwards, will the story change? +
In theory, the plot might get really confusing! While it’s a fun idea, it’s probably best to read books front to back—unless you enjoy puzzling over plot holes!
If aliens visit Earth, what’s the best way to greet them? +
With a cup of coffee and a “Welcome to Earth!” sign. If they’re anything like us, they might appreciate a good caffeine boost.
